About Us

Insurance Management Services Limited (IMS), is an Insurance Brokerage firm registered under Ghana Laws and operates under the Insurance Act, 2006 (Act 724). We are also registered with the National Insurance Commission of Ghana and the Insurance Brokers Association of Ghana.

IMS offers risk management and advisory services to individuals and corporate entities by acting as an intermediary between our clients and insurance companies in Ghana.

Our area of expertise and experience lies specifically in the assessment of risk and providing the best cover possible at affordable premiums to our clients. This gives our clients the peace of mind to concentrate on their core functions knowing very well all possible areas of risks in their business have the best Insurance cover.

Building long term relationships with our clients is something IMS strongly believes in. To this end, we go to great lengths to ensure our valuable clients receive first-rate service.
