Third Party Policy

  • This is the minimum cover allowed under the ACT 1958 with which you can drive a vehicle in Ghana.
  • It covers Injury, death and property damage to third parties i.e. persons in a car which impacts with your car or pedestrians who are knocked down due to negligence on your part or on the part of your driver.
  • The minimum limit for third-party property damage at present is GH¢5000, However this can be increased at your request.
  • It does not cover other accidental damage to your vehicle itself.
  • It does not cover Theft or damage resulting from fire.
  • It does not cover damage to windshield and others that may be considered minor.

Comprehensive Policy

  • This is the minimum cover allowed under the ACT 1958 with which you can drive a vehicle in Ghana.

Third Party Fire & Theft

  • This is a Third Party Policy that also covers your car against fire and theft.
  • It does not cover other accidental damage to your vehicle itself.
  • It does not cover damage to windshield and others that may be considered minor.

Third Party Amplified (Enhanced Motor Third Party)

  • This is available for customers on third party only or Third-Party Fire & Theft policy.
  • It covers only windscreen, side-mirrors, and minor scratches at an affordable price.
  • It does not cover other accidental damage to your vehicle itself.

Motor Comprehensive (CompExtra)

  • This is a Comprehensive policy that has extra benefit for Loss of use , Compensation for mechanical breakdown resulting from misfuelling and Ridesharing (P.A. cover extended to 4 additional persons travelling in same car)
